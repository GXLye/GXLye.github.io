Redle Helpline

If you are feeling ill now please contact your GP.

If you’re ill when your doctor’s surgery is closed and you feel it can’t wait until it re-opens, call us at Redle 24 on 08-4542424. Calls to us should cost no more than the price of a local telephone call from a BT landline. Calls from mobiles and other networks may vary.

Alternatively, you can send us your health issues through our electronic form here


ESO Helpline

Our partner service, the ESO inform Helpline, can give you details of all pharmacies, GP practices and dental practices in your area. They can also give you information about illnesses and conditions, treatments, NHS services and other support services.

Telephone: 1300-88-2525 (8am to 10pm, 7 days).


Our centres

You can get the address of our head office, together with local centre information by calling us or by emailing us at support@redle.com


Comments about Redle

If you wish to make a comment or complaint you can speak directly to a member of staff in Redle 24. Staff will be pleased to discuss your concerns with you and may be able to deal with matters straight away. Call 08-4542525 (9am to 5pm, Monday to Friday).
