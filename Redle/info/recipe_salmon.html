Pan-Grilled Salmon with Pineapple Salsa

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chopped fresh pineapple
  • 2 tablespoons chopped onion
  • 2 tablespoons cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground pepper
  • Cooking spray
  • 4 (6-ounce) salmon fillets
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Prepare Time: 8 minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 minutes
  • Yield: 2 serving

Preparation

  1. Combine first 5 ingredients (through pepper) in a bowl; set aside.
  2. Heat a nonstick grill pan coated with cooking spray over medium-high heat. Sprinkle fish with salt.
  3. Cook fish 4 minutes on each side or until it flakes easily when tested with a fork. Top with salsa.
